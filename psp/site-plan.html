Purpose

This website is a base for my final project, that will display my game, and show off different skills The main idea of this website is to show off a game that has been a personal project for me. The audience that I have will be my classmates, as well as my professor. But the main idea here is people who are following my ideas for the game. The purpose of the website is to also display easy to understand information. And lots of pictures and videos that show off the game. The website will showoff lots of details, and questions and answers that main be generally asked about the game, release date, size, etc. A large amount of the screenshots and details will show off how to navigate the site, as well as work on making it easy to understand. Another large point of this site will help people not have to read a lot of details, but quick and easy inforamtion that they can connect together.
